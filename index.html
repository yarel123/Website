Soccer

What is Soccer?

Soccer, also known as football, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players using a round ball with the objective of scoring more goals than the opposing team by kicking or heading the ball into the opponent's goal.

History of Soccer

The modern version of soccer originated in Britain in the mid-19th century, with the first official rules being published in 1863. The game has since become one of the most popular sports in the world, with millions of players and fans globally.
